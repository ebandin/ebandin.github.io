Inspiration

I believe that a good measure of a person's character is to find out what drives them.
I am powered by innovation. I try to make it so that new experiences and lessons are a constant in my life. I live for the opportunity to explore the world from muse to muse. Some examples these oasis' of inspiration are as follows:
1) Poi in all of it's forms as the practice of flow and inner harmony.
2) Your dreams. My dreams. A pet's dreams. All dreams can be good dreams if considered critically and applied positively.

3)A good story. Whether I'm on the Alligator beat or kicking back with friends my eyes are always open for a good tale.
